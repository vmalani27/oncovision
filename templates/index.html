What is Skin Cancer?

Skin cancer is the abnormal growth of skin cells, most often developing on skin exposed to the sun. It's the most common type of cancer worldwide, but when caught early, it's also one of the most treatable.

The three main types of skin cancer are:

  • Basal cell carcinoma (BCC)
  • Squamous cell carcinoma (SCC)
  • Melanoma

Risk Factors

Understanding your risk factors can help you take preventive measures:

  • Fair skin that burns easily
  • Family history of skin cancer
  • Excessive sun exposure
  • History of sunburns
  • Moles or unusual growths
  • Weakened immune system
